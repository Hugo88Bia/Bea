Get the best voice
For a much friendlier reading voice, download an enhanced voice once on this device:

1. Open Settings
2. Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices → English
3. Pick a voice marked Enhanced or Premium (or a Siri voice) and tap to download

Then come back — the app will use it automatically. It stays free and works offline.
